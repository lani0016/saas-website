Welcome to Our Website!

Here at Bean bag conferencing, we cater to those who are just starting out with their need for a conferencing service. Who you may ask? College kids! Like many young adults, hectic lives may leave one tired and overwhelmed. That is why our website has the simplest ways to help students survive the daily grind; from chat groups, to note swapping and tutorials, students can build themselves for success in ways that a classroom may not be able to provide on its own. Plus, with the innovative ability to post links to any and all crowd funding websites, we know those who come together to use our site will be well taken care of if they need to reach out to our community for a boost!
