Welcome to Our Website!

Here at Bean bag conferencing, we cater to those who are just starting out with their need for a conferencing service.Who you may ask? College kids! Like many young adults, hectic lives may leave one tired and overwhelmed. That is why our website has the simplest ways to help students survive the daily grind; from chat groups, to note swapping and tutorials, students can build themselves for success in ways that a classroom may not be able to provide on its own. Plus, with the innovative ability to post links to any and all crowd funding websites, we know those who come together to use our site will be well taken care of if they need to reach out to our community for a boost!

  1. Video tutorials

    Need the new details on how to do something? Having difficulty following lessons? Or just plain want to expand on what you know? This section has links to a variety of trusted video sources to bring you good quality videos with appropriate content for your viewing convenience!

  2. Chatrooms

    Here we have our chatroom services! This is for when you need to get filled in on a subject from some people you know, or want some help in a subject and want responses with texting fast response time. Either way, when using chatrooms we pride ourselves with keeping them clean, and content oriented.

  3. Note swap

    So you’ve gotten a test you need to pass or just want to be prepared in case you lose your books? Look no further! Note swap has you covered. Simply look up what subject you study, and browse the gallery for the notes you need and print from the scan! The only catch is that you need to upload at least one page of notes yourself so that we can keep some variety and relevant information on our website and keep the instances of plagiarism as low as possible.

  4. Crowd funding

    Need to get a boost to make it to the end of the week? The month? Or need to save up for your next course and have tried everything but still need that last push to make the deadline? Just post a link to your crowd funder of choice and you could be getting donations within the hour!
